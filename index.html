SCCSelf-custody Consensus

SCC leverages cryptographic technologies such as Taproot, MPC, and accountable assertions, ensuring that Bitcoin-native asset control remains with the user while preventing arbitrary termination of custody, thereby eliminating the need for trust between parties or third-party intermediaries.
